Who Are We?

We are a group of Youth Activists based in Warren, New Jersey. We cannot sit back anymore and watch children die in schools. We cannot let kids be scared of their schools. We just cannot sit back and do nothing. We created We Rise NJ as a way for others to show their concern with this issue and help create positive change in our society.

Make a Difference

Protest

Protest is an extremely effective way to raise awareness for a cause, especially when executed correctly. In order for gun violence prevention legislation to be passed, it must remain a part of the national conversation. We must constantly remind our elected officials know how important of an issue gun violence is to their constituents. Large protests are great ways to do this.

Discuss

Engaging in conversation about gun violence prevention is essential to the cause. Reaching out to your representatives, vocalizing your support, and attending town halls are productive ways to discuss gun violence prevention.

Petition

Petition your elected officials. If they know that a lot of their constituents, or even their soon-to-be constituents, support gun violence prevention, that puts pressure on them to consider it.

Get Knowledgeable

Stay informed. The strongest champion of a cause is the kind that is extremely knowledgeable about it.
